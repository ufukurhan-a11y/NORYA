# Blog 10 Konu — Hero Görsel & İkon Konsepti (Teslim)

Her konu için hero image prompt ve icon concept. Görseller konu ile birebir alakalı, birbirine çok benzemeyecek, Norya marka hissi korunacak.

---

## 1. Albumin Low (albumin-low-meaning)

- **Hero image prompt:** Soft gradient background (light teal to white). Single clear droplet or minimal serum vial silhouette, no face. Clean medical-infographic style; no stock-photo people. Subtle protein/molecule hint in background. Premium, calm, trustworthy. Norya teal accent allowed.
- **Icon concept:** Minimal droplet or single curved line suggesting fluid/protein. Not a heart, not a liver icon.

---

## 2. ALP High (alp-high-meaning)

- **Hero image prompt:** Abstract representation of enzyme activity: soft geometric shapes suggesting bone and liver (neutral tones, no anatomy). Light gradient (warm white to very light amber). Clean, modern, no text. Medical-tech feel, not cartoon.
- **Icon concept:** Stylised upward trend or gentle “activity” curve (ALP elevation). No literal bone or liver shape.

---

## 3. Sodium Low (sodium-low-meaning)

- **Hero image prompt:** Minimal balance or scale in soft focus; very subtle water/sodium metaphor (droplets or soft waves). Cool white and light blue-grey palette. No faces, no pills. Premium, calm.
- **Icon concept:** Simple balance or two gentle curves suggesting equilibrium/electrolyte. Distinct from albumin droplet.

---

## 4. Potassium High (potassium-high-meaning)

- **Hero image prompt:** Soft abstract shapes suggesting rhythm or wave (heart/kidney association without literal organs). Muted green-teal and white. Clean, infographic style. No fruit, no bananas.
- **Icon concept:** Gentle wave or rhythm line (electrolyte/balance). Not a heart symbol.

---

## 5. Calcium High (calcium-high-meaning)

- **Hero image prompt:** Abstract “structure” or crystalline hint in very soft focus; light grey and white with one subtle teal accent. No bones, no milk. Professional, calm.
- **Icon concept:** Minimal structure or crystal-inspired simple shape. Not a bone icon.

---

## 6. Lymphocytes High or Low (lymphocytes-high-or-low)

- **Hero image prompt:** Abstract cluster of soft circles (suggesting cells) in gradient; one tone slightly emphasised. White and light blue-grey. Immune/cell theme without literal microscope. Clean, premium.
- **Icon concept:** Two or three overlapping circles (cell cluster). Distinct from monocyte single-cell idea.

---

## 7. Monocytes High (monocytes-high-meaning)

- **Hero image prompt:** Single soft circular form (cell suggestion) with subtle inner detail; muted purple-grey and white. No microscope, no blood. Calm, medical-tech.
- **Icon concept:** Single circle with one soft inner ring or dot (one cell type). Different from lymphocyte cluster.

---

## 8. MCV High or Low (mcv-high-or-low)

- **Hero image prompt:** Abstract “size” metaphor: two soft shapes of different sizes (e.g. circles) on neutral background. Light teal and white. Red cell theme without literal blood. Clean, minimal.
- **Icon concept:** Two circles—one smaller, one larger—suggesting cell volume. No ruler, no needle.

---

## 9. RDW High (rdw-high-meaning)

- **Hero image prompt:** Subtle “spread” or variation metaphor: several soft circles with slight size variation on light grey-white gradient. No chart, no graph. Calm, professional.
- **Icon concept:** Three to four circles with slight size difference (variation in size). Distinct from MCV two-size only.

---

## 10. A/G Ratio High or Low (ag-ratio-high-or-low)

- **Hero image prompt:** Minimal ratio/balance visual: two soft bands or blocks in different shades (e.g. white and light grey) with clear but subtle proportion. No fractions, no math symbols. Clean, trustworthy.
- **Icon concept:** Two horizontal bars or segments suggesting ratio (A vs G). Simple, not a pie chart.

---

## Genel kurallar (yayın öncesi)

- Her hero görsel konu ile birebir alakalı.
- Görseller ve ikonlar birbirine çok benzemeyecek; aynı görsel hissi tekrarlanmayacak.
- Norya marka hissi (premium, clean, medical-tech, güven) korunacak.
- CTA metinleri blog sonunda doğal; satış baskısı yok. Analyze ve pricing sayfalarına yönlendirme metin içinde veya son blokta.
