The Vision

Ufuk Urhan founded NoryaAI on January 1, 2018, with a clear and personal mission: blood test results should be written for humans, not just for machines. Too many people receive lab reports filled with numbers and abbreviations they cannot understand, and too few have immediate access to a doctor who can explain them.

Building NoryaAI

Starting from Hamburg, Germany, Ufuk built NoryaAI from the ground up — designing the AI analysis engine, building partnerships with healthcare institutions, and expanding the platform to support 9+ report languages. What began as a single-person project has grown into a platform used by 120+ hospitals and clinics worldwide.
