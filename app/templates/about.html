NoryaAI was born from a personal challenge: navigating blood test results across different countries, languages, and healthcare systems. As someone managing a chronic autoimmune condition, I experienced firsthand the confusion and anxiety that comes with incomprehensible lab reports.

Since 2018, I've been building NoryaAI with a clear mission: to make laboratory results understandable for everyone, regardless of their medical background or language. Our platform combines advanced AI technology with rigorous medical oversight to provide clear, actionable health insights.

Expertise & Background

  • ▸ 8+ years building health technology platforms with focus on laboratory data interpretation
  • ▸ Deep expertise in biomarker analysis, clinical laboratory standards, and medical informatics
  • ▸ Personal lived experience with chronic autoimmune condition management across multiple healthcare systems
  • ▸ Based in Hamburg, Germany; serving users in 50+ countries
