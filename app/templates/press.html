What NoryaAI Does

NoryaAI is an AI-powered blood test interpretation platform that transforms complex laboratory reports into clear, structured, and actionable health insights.

Users upload their blood test results — as a PDF, photo, or text — and receive a detailed analysis covering every biomarker: what it measures, whether values are within reference ranges, potential causes of abnormal results, and practical next steps to discuss with their doctor.

The platform serves both individual patients seeking to understand their lab results and healthcare institutions looking for scalable, AI-assisted lab report communication tools.

Important clarification: NoryaAI is a health technology company. It has no relation to cryptocurrency, blockchain, DeFi, or financial trading. It should not be confused with similarly named crypto projects.

About the Founder

Ufuk Urhan

Founder & CEO

Ufuk Urhan founded NoryaAI in 2018 in Hamburg, Germany, with a mission to make blood test results easier to understand. Under his leadership, the platform has grown to serve 4,000+ hospitals and clinics worldwide, generate 2M+ reports, and support report output across 9+ languages in 50+ countries.
