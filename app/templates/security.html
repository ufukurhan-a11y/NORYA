FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How is my health data encrypted?

All data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit. Each user's biomarker profile is stored in its own isolated encrypted container with unique encryption keys managed by Hardware Security Modules (HSM). Even in the unlikely event of a system breach, individual data containers remain impenetrable.

Can NoryaAI employees see my lab results?

No. We use a zero-knowledge architecture where the AI processes your data within an encrypted sandbox. Raw laboratory values are never exposed to human operators. Our staff can only see anonymized metadata necessary for system maintenance—never your actual health data.

How can I delete all my data?

You can request permanent deletion of your entire account and all associated data at any time. Under GDPR's "Right to be Forgotten," we don't just hide your data—we purge it from all servers globally, including backups, within 30 days of your request. This process is irreversible and complete.

Is my payment information secure?

We never store your credit card details. All payment processing is handled by PayTR, a PCI-DSS Level 1 certified payment provider. Transactions are tokenized and encrypted, ensuring your financial data never touches our servers.

Where is my data physically stored?

Your data is stored in geographically distributed, SOC 2 certified data centers with automated redundancy. All storage locations comply with GDPR and KVKK regulations. Data residency preferences can be configured for enterprise customers to meet specific regulatory requirements.

Do you share data with third parties?

Absolutely not. Your health data is never shared with, sold to, or accessed by any third party. We do not use your data for advertising, research, or any purpose beyond providing you with personalized health insights. Our business model is built on subscriptions, not data monetization.
